## Slide 2 — Problem Framing

**Header**: Why current infrastructure fails autonomous agents

Key points:
- identity gap (agent attribution)
- delegation gap (all-or-nothing credentials)
- auditability gap (why decision happened)
- operations gap (machine-speed supervision)

Visual suggestion:
- 2-column table: "today vs agentic reality"

## Slide 3 — Product Thesis

**Header**: What AOA is (and is not)

Key points:
- AOA is a trust/control layer
- not a neobank, not a standalone wallet
- objective: safe, bounded, auditable execution

On-slide quote:
- "We do not replace banks. We make agentic execution governable."

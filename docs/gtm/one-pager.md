# Agentic Bank — Executive One-Pager

## The Problem

AI agents are executing billions of dollars in transactions, but they lack proper financial infrastructure. Today's agents operate through human-owned accounts with no identity, no risk controls, and no audit trail. This creates regulatory, security, and operational risk at scale.

## The Solution

Agentic Bank provides **bank-grade financial infrastructure purpose-built for autonomous AI agents**. We give every agent its own Account of Agency (AOA) with identity verification, capability-scoped permissions, and real-time risk controls — all inside a MAS Regulatory Sandbox.

## 7-Layer Agentic Banking Stack

| Layer | Capability |
|-------|-----------|
| 1. KYA Identity | SPIFFE/SVID-based agent identity (Know Your Agent) |
| 2. Token Delegation | Capability tokens with scoped permissions and expiry |
| 3. Policy Engine | Deny-by-default OPA policy evaluation |
| 4. Risk Engine | 15 pre-trade risk rules with real-time scoring |
| 5. Settlement Rail | SGD settlement with atomic commit |
| 6. Compliance | AML screening and Travel Rule compliance |
| 7. Audit | Immutable decision records for every transaction |

## Key Metrics

- **Settlement success rate**: >99.5%
- **Risk evaluation latency**: <50ms p99

## 4. Recommended Engagement Strategy

### 4.1 Phase 1: Immediate (Weeks 1-2)

| Action | Recommended Partner | Rationale |
|--------|--------------------|-----------| 
| **Legal counsel RFP** | Send to Allen & Gledhill + Rajah & Tann + WongPartnership | Get 3 proposals; select on PSA experience, team, fees |
| **Conflict check** | All shortlisted firms | Ensure no conflicts with SC Group or competitors |
| **Scope of engagement** | Combined: ACRA incorporation + PSA SPI license + MAS sandbox application | Single firm for all regulatory work reduces coordination cost |

### 4.2 Phase 1: Weeks 3-4

| Action | Recommended Partner | Rationale |
|--------|--------------------|-----------| 
| **Appoint legal counsel** | Winner of RFP | Target: signed engagement letter by Week 4 |
| **Appoint corporate secretary** | Boardroom or Tricor | Can be recommended by appointed law firm |
| **Engage accounting firm** | KPMG or PwC | Shortlist based on existing SC Group audit relationship |

### 4.3 Phase 2: Month 2+

| Action | Partner | Rationale |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **AML/CFT framework** | Accounting firm (advisory arm) | Framework design before sandbox application |
| **Tax structuring** | PwC (if not audit partner) | Intercompany pricing, Singapore tax incentives |
| **Statutory audit engagement** | Select non-advisory Big Four firm | Independence: auditor should not also be primary advisor |

## 6. Selection Criteria

When evaluating proposals, weight these factors:

| Criterion | Weight | Rationale |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **PSA/MAS sandbox experience** | 30% | Non-negotiable; firm must have done this before |
| **Team seniority & availability** | 25% | Senior partner involvement; not just junior associates |
| **Fee competitiveness** | 20% | Value for money within market range |
| **SC/SCV relationship** | 15% | Existing relationship reduces onboarding friction |
| **Multi-market capability** | 10% | Phase 2 expansion to HK/ASEAN; same firm preferred |
